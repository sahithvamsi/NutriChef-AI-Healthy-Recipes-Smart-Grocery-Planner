👨‍🍳 Pro Cooking Tips

🥬 Use Fresh Ingredients

Fresh vegetables, herbs, and proteins enhance flavor, texture, and nutritional value. Always check color, smell, and firmness before purchasing.

🔥 Preheat Your Pan

A properly heated pan ensures even cooking and prevents sticking. Always allow your pan to heat before adding oil or ingredients.

🧂 Balance Salt Carefully

Add salt gradually and taste frequently. Proper seasoning enhances natural flavors without overpowering the dish.

🍖 Let Meat Rest

Allow cooked meat to rest 5–10 minutes before cutting. This keeps juices inside and improves tenderness.

🥘 Master Knife Skills

Uniform cutting ensures even cooking. Invest time in learning chopping, dicing, and julienne techniques.

⏳ Control Cooking Time

Overcooking destroys texture and nutrients. Follow timing closely and use timers for precision.
