🥦 Mastering Advanced Vegetarian Cooking

Vegetarian cuisine is more than just removing meat. It focuses on maximizing flavor, nutrition, and creativity using plant-based ingredients.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Protein sources like lentils, chickpeas, tofu, and paneer
  • Balancing macronutrients for complete meals
  • Using spices to enhance natural flavors
  • Seasonal vegetable pairing techniques

Advanced Techniques:

Roasting vegetables at high heat, fermentation (kimchi, pickles), and slow-cooked legumes improve depth of flavor and digestibility.
