🍗 High-Performance Non‑Vegetarian Cooking

Non-vegetarian cuisine provides high-quality protein and essential nutrients like iron, zinc, and B12.

Smart Cooking Strategies:

  • Grilling instead of deep frying
  • Marinating to improve tenderness
  • Temperature control for perfect doneness
  • Balancing meat with fiber-rich vegetables

Nutritional Focus:

Lean meats such as chicken breast and fish support muscle growth and recovery. Proper portion control is key for balanced nutrition.
