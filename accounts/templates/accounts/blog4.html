🏋️ Smart Fitness & Healthy Lifestyle Cooking

Healthy cooking is about balance, portion control, and nutrient timing. It supports long-term wellness and performance.

Core Principles:

  • High-protein balanced meals
  • Low processed sugar intake
  • Healthy fats (nuts, olive oil, seeds)
  • Hydration and electrolyte balance

Meal Planning Strategy:

Prepare meals in advance to avoid unhealthy food choices. Focus on whole foods and mindful eating.
